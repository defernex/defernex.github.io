TOMMASO de FERNEX

FRG MINI-WORKSHOP - FALL 2013

Cremona groups

The goal of this workshop is to learn about the following recent papers

  • Topologies and structures of the Cremona groups by Jérémy Blanc and Jean-Philippe Furter
  • Normal subgroups in the Cremona group by Serge Cantat and Stéphane Lamy

Venue

University of Utah, November 22-24, 2013

All talks are in LCB Builing of the Math Department, Room 225. During the weekend the glass door will be closed but the main door on the front of the building will be open.

Please refer to the interactive campus map to locate the LCB Building as well as the University Guest House.

To reach campus or the Guest House from the airport, taxis are about $25. Alternatively, there is the Trax (a city train) which run from the airport to downton, where you change to another Trax train that brings you to campus and the Guest House (please refer to the above link, or to the map at the Trax station for directions).
