RESEARCH

My research is in algebraic geometry. I study singularities using methods from birational geometry, non-archimedean geometry, and differential geometry. Specific tools include arc spaces and multiplier ideals. I have also worked on questions of rationality of algebraic varities, geometry of Fano manifolds, and Cremona groups.
